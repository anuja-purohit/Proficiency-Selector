About Us

Jaypee University of Information Technology is a private university located in Himachal Pradesh, India. It was established in 2002 and offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral courses in engineering, management, and computer science. The university is known for its excellent academic and research facilities, highly qualified faculty, and student-centered approach to education.
